The Flag of Nigeria

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
This article needs additional citations for verification. Please help improve this article by additing citations to reliable sources. Unsecured material may be challenged and removed.

The flag of the Federal Republic of Nigeria was designed in 1959 and first officially hoisted on 1 October 1960. The flag has three vertical bands of green, white, green. The two green stripes represent natural wealth, and the white represents peace and unity.[1][2]
